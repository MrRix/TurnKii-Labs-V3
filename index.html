What exactly is TurnKii Labs?

TurnKii labs allows you to try new features that have not been released yet. These new features may change, break, or even disappear sometimes. Your input is extremely important to us and will shape how the final product looks, acts, and solves your problems.

Features we are currently testing

Bill Consolidation and Reminders

  • Keep track of your utility bills all-in-one place, automatically.

  • Receive email or text reminders a few days before each bill is due. Never fear missing a payment again.

Do I Need to be a Landlord?

  • Not at all. The Bill Consolidation feature is for anyone who pays utility bills.

  • No Personal or Payment information is collected, stored, or transmitted as part of our platform.
